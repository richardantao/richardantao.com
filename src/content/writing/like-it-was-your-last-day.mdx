What would you do if today was your last day?

> Smell the roses like it was your last day.

> Watch the sunset like it was your last day.

> Sing to the birds like it was your last day.

> Take that walk like it was your last day.

> Exercise like it was your last day.

> Drive home with the radio like it was your last day.

> Make the trip like it was your last day.

> Dance like it was your last day.

> Read that book like it was your last day.

> Enjoy that meal like it was your last day.

> Listen to the wise man like it was your last day.

> Listen to the fool like it was your last day.

> Call your friend like it was your last day.

> Kiss her like it was your last day.

> Hold him tight like it was your last day.

*Because one day, it will be.*
