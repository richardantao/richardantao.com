What would you do if today was your last day?

> Smell the roses like it was your last day.<br />
> Watch the sunset like it was your last day.<br />
> Sing to the birds like it was your last day.<br />
> Take that walk like it was your last day.<br />
> Exercise like it was your last day.<br />
> Drive home with the radio like it was your last day.<br />
> Make the trip like it was your last day.<br />
> Dance like it was your last day.<br />
> Read that book like it was your last day.<br />
> Enjoy that meal like it was your last day.<br />
> Listen to the wise man like it was your last day.<br />
> Listen to the fool like it was your last day.<br />
> Forgive like it was your last day.<br />
> Call your friend like it was your last day.<br />
> Kiss her like it was your last day.<br />
> Hold him tight like it was your last day.<br />
> Love like it was your last day.

*Because one day, it will be.*
